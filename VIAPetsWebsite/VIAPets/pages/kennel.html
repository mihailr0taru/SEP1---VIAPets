Kennel Page

Welcome to our kennel services!

Welcome to VIAPets Kennel Services, where your pet is treated as a Very Important Animal! Whether you're traveling, attending business trips, or simply need a reliable place for your furry friend, VIAPets offers a cozy, caring environment that ensures the comfort and well-being of each pet. Our team of experienced caregivers is dedicated to creating a home-away-from-home for cats, dogs, and birds. With a commitment to safety and love for animals, VIAPets is the perfect choice to provide the best care for your beloved companion.

How our kennel services work?

  • Check Availability: (Click here to check availability!) On our website, below this page, you can see the number of open spots in our kennel.
  • Get in Touch: Contact us by phone or visit our shop to confirm availability and reserve a spot.
  • Provide Pet Details: Share essential information about your pet, including breed, age, and specific needs, so we can cater to them accordingly.
  • Book Your Dates: Schedule your pet's stay based on your travel plans, ensuring a comfortable experience.
  • Confirm and Drop Off: Finalize the booking and drop off your pet on the agreed date. Our team will give them a warm welcome and make them feel at home.
