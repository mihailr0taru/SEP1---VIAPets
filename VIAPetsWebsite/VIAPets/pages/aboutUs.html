Originally known as “Pets,” our store found its footing during the pandemic, when many people realized that a pet could bring comfort and companionship. Mr. Bob recognized this need and expanded VIAPets to include kennel services for pet owners on the go. When you’re away, your pet is more than just looked after – they’re treated like family. Here, every animal is a VIA: a Very Important Animal. The name embodies our vision. Here, every creature, whether it’s a lively puppy, a wise old turtle, or a vibrant parrot, is treated with the utmost care and respect.



What sets us apart!

"100% Recycled" Pets – A New Way to Adopt

At VIAPets, we know that sometimes circumstances change, and pet owners may need to rehome their furry friends. Mr. Bob saw an opportunity to turn this into something positive. Every dog, cat, or bird in our shop is 100% recycled – a gentle nod to sustainability, giving pets a second chance with new owners who’ll love them.

Our Community, Our Values

As the only pet shop in town with combined kennel services, we set ourselves apart with a blend of quality, care, and convenience. Mr. Bob and the team have a strong local following, and whether it’s pet owners seeking supplies or locals wanting to adopt, we’re here to build relationships that matter. And with plans for more services in the future, like pet food and a dedicated website, VIAPets continues to grow, bringing new offerings to our beloved community.
